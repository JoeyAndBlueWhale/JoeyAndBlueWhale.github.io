About Me

I’m a third year PhD student at UT Austin under the supervision of Eric Price. Before I was a Master student at UBC and was co-supervised by Nick Harvey and Mark Schmidt.

Recently I’m studying active learning. Specifically, we’re working on designing Bayesian active learning methods with near-optimal theoretical gurantees. During my Master’s studies, my research revolved around first-order optimization methods and online learning. I am also interested in data-driven algorithms and differential privacy. More generally, I work on learning theory and like TCS problems. If you want to collaborate or even just chat with me, please send me an email!
